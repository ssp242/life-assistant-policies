Life Assistant

A personal, single-user assistant.

Life Assistant is a private tool built and used by a single individual (the developer) for personal productivity: a daily WhatsApp brief (weather, calendar, news, inbox summary) and on-demand replies, with read/write access to the developer's own Gmail and Google Calendar.

It is not a public product, is not available for signup, and is not used by anyone other than its developer.
